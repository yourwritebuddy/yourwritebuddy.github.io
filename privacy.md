# Privacy Policy

At FreeWriteChecker, we value your privacy and are committed to maintaining the security of your data.

This website provides writing tools that operate entirely within your browser environment. Please be aware of the following regarding your data:

* **No Data Storage:** We do not store, save, or transmit any of the text you enter into our tools to any external servers. Your writing remains private and is only processed locally on your own device.
* **Local Processing:** All calculations, such as word counts or character analysis, happen locally in your web browser. Once you close your browser tab or refresh the page, your input text is cleared.
* **Anonymous Usage:** We do not track individual users or associate personal profiles with the use of these tools. Your experience on this site is intended to be completely anonymous and secure.

By using our tools, you agree that your content is handled locally on your own hardware, ensuring you remain in full control of your information at all times.
